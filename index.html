Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CarbonSociety and how it can benefit your car club.

What is CarbonSociety?

CarbonSociety is a comprehensive car club management platform that helps organizers plan events, manage members, and build stronger automotive communities. From local meets to track days, we provide all the tools you need to run a successful car club.

How do I create my first event?

Creating events is simple! Just log into your dashboard, click "New Event," fill in the details like date, time, and location, and invite your members. Our system handles RSVPs, notifications, and attendance tracking automatically.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your club data will remain accessible until the end of your billing period, and you can reactivate whenever you're ready.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! CarbonSociety is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile devices. We also have native iOS and Android apps in development that will be available soon.

How does member management work?

Our member management system lets you track member profiles, their cars, attendance history, and engagement levels. Members can update their own profiles and car information, while you maintain control over club settings and permissions.

Can I import my existing member list?

Absolutely! We provide easy import tools for common formats like CSV and Excel. Our support team can also help you migrate from other platforms to ensure a smooth transition.
